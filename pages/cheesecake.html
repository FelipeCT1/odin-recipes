Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

  • 18 Graham crackers, crushed
  • 3 Tablespoons of melted butter

Filling

  • 4 Packages of Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 1/2 Cups of white sugar
  • 4 Large eggs

Instructions

Preheat your oven at 175ÂºC

Crust

  1. Mix the Graham cracker crumbs with the butter until evenly mixed.
  2. Press this mixture into the bottom of a stringform pan.

Filling

  1. In a bowl, whisk the Cream cheese, sugar, eggs and sour cream until the mixture becomes smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into your pan with the buttered crumbs
  3. Bake until the edges of the filling have puffed slightly, yet the center of it should still jiggle.
  4. Turn off the oven, and let the Cheesecake cool for at least 3 hours.
  5. Serve as you wish, traditionally with strawberry jam.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup unsalted butter
  • 1 Cup white sugar
  • 1/2 Cup brown sugar
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 Tablespoons milk
  • 1 Teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 1/4 Cups of AP flour
  • 1 1/2 Cups of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat the butter along with the white sugarand brown sugar, until fluffy. Add the baking soda, vanilla extract and egg.
  2. Add the flour, mix it in with a spatula until it forms a cohesive dough. Add in the chocolate chips
  3. Let the dough rest for 1 hour. In the meantime, preheat your oven at 190ÂºC
  4. In a tray, scoop roughly 1 tablespoon per cookie. Be cautious with the spacing, the cookies will spread!
  5. Bake them until golden brown.
  6. Turn off your oven and let the cookies cool down before eating/storing
